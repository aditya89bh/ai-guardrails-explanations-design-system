# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful experience for everyone, regardless of level of experience, background, or identity.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome different perspectives and technical backgrounds
- Accept feedback gracefully and give feedback kindly
- Focus discussions on the technical merits of contributions
- Respect maintainer decisions, even when you disagree

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Dismissing contributors based on their identity or experience level
- Sustained disruptive behavior in discussions or reviews

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, and any other project communication channels.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainers through the GitHub Security Advisory mechanism (for private reporting).

Reports will be reviewed and responded to. Maintainers will take whatever action they consider appropriate, up to and including removing comments, closing issues, or banning participants from the project.
